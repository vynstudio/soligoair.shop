Your Home's Air May Be More Polluted Than You Think

Florida's high humidity creates the perfect environment for mold, mildew, and dust mites to thrive inside your home. Add pet dander, pollen, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from everyday household products, and the air you breathe indoors can be two to five times more polluted than outdoor air.

Soligo Air's indoor air quality solutions tackle these invisible threats at the source. From whole-home air purification to humidity control, we design systems that work with your existing HVAC to deliver cleaner, healthier air to every room in your home.

Our IAQ Solutions

Air Purification Systems

Whole-home HEPA filtration and electronic air cleaners that capture 99.97% of airborne particles — including dust, pollen, pet dander, and smoke. Installed directly into your ductwork for seamless, silent operation.

UV Germicidal Lights

Ultraviolet light systems installed inside your ductwork and near the evaporator coil to kill mold, bacteria, and viruses before they circulate through your home. A proven technology used in hospitals and clean rooms.

Humidity Control

Whole-home dehumidifiers designed for Florida's relentless humidity. Maintain ideal indoor humidity between 40–60% to prevent mold growth, reduce allergens, and keep your home comfortable without overcooling.

Duct Cleaning & Sealing

Remove years of dust, debris, and biological buildup from your ductwork and seal leaks that waste energy and pull in unfiltered air from attics and crawl spaces. Better airflow, better air quality.

Benefits of Better Indoor Air Quality

Reduce Allergens

Remove dust mites, pollen, pet dander, and mold spores that trigger allergies, asthma, and respiratory issues — especially important for families with children and seniors.

Eliminate Odors

Get rid of lingering cooking smells, pet odors, and musty air caused by mold and mildew. UV lights and air purifiers neutralize odor-causing particles at the molecular level.

Protect Your HVAC Investment

Cleaner air means cleaner coils, ducts, and filters. IAQ solutions reduce strain on your system, lower energy bills, and extend the life of your air conditioner.
