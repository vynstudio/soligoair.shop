Is It Time for a New AC System?

If your air conditioner is over 10-15 years old, it may be costing you more in repairs and energy bills than a new, high-efficiency system would. Here are the top signs it's time to replace:

  • System age: Units older than 10-15 years lose efficiency and break down more often.
  • Frequent repairs: If repair costs exceed 50% of a new unit's price, replacement is the smarter investment.
  • R-22 refrigerant: R-22 (Freon) has been phased out, making older systems expensive to recharge.
  • Rising energy bills: An aging AC works harder to cool your home, driving up electricity costs every month.
