Keep Your AC Running at Peak Performance

Florida's relentless heat and humidity put extreme demands on your air conditioning system. Without regular maintenance, efficiency drops, energy bills climb, and small issues silently turn into expensive breakdowns.

An annual tune-up is the single best investment you can make for your HVAC system. Our EPA-certified technicians perform a comprehensive inspection and cleaning that catches worn parts, refrigerant leaks, and electrical problems before they leave you sweating in the middle of summer. What's Included in Our $89 Tune-Up

Thermostat Calibration

We verify your thermostat reads accurately and communicates properly with your system, ensuring consistent comfort throughout your home.

Refrigerant Level Check

Low refrigerant means your AC works harder and cools less. We measure levels and check for leaks to keep efficiency at its best.

Coil Cleaning

Dirty evaporator and condenser coils reduce cooling capacity and drive up energy costs. We clean them for maximum heat transfer.

Electrical Connection Inspection

Loose or corroded wiring is a safety hazard and a leading cause of system failure. We tighten connections and test voltage.

Drain Line Clearing

Clogged condensate drain lines cause water damage and mold growth. We flush and clear the line to prevent costly leaks.

Full System Performance Test

We measure airflow, temperature differential, and overall system output to confirm your AC is running at peak performance.

Benefits of Regular AC Maintenance

Lower Energy Bills

A well-maintained AC uses up to 15% less energy than a neglected one. Our tune-up restores efficiency and keeps your monthly costs down.

Fewer Breakdowns

Most emergency repairs are caused by issues that could have been caught during routine maintenance. Prevention is always cheaper than repair.

Extended System Life

The average AC lasts 10–15 years, but regular maintenance can add 5+ years to your system's lifespan — saving thousands in replacement costs.
