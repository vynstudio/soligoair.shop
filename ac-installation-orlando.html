Time for a New System?

Signs Your AC Needs Replacing

If your system is doing any of these, replacement is usually the smarter long-term move.

10+ Years Old

Most systems lose major efficiency after 10–15 years. Repairs only delay the inevitable.

Rising Energy Bills

An aging AC works harder to cool your home. Bills creep up month after month.

Frequent Repairs

If you're calling a tech every season, replacement is usually cheaper than chasing breakdowns.

Uses R-22 Refrigerant

R-22 (Freon) has been phased out. Recharging older systems is expensive and rarely worth it.

Uneven Cooling

Hot and cold spots usually mean an undersized or failing system that can't keep up.

Loud or Noisy Operation

Banging, grinding, or rattling means major components are failing — usually past the repair window.
