Common Questions

HVAC FAQ

Should I repair or replace my AC?

If your system is under 10 years old and the repair cost is less than 50% of a new system, repair is usually the smart move. We give you an honest recommendation either way — no pressure.

How fast can you get a tech to my home?

In most cases, we offer same-day service across Orlando and Central Florida. Call before noon for the best chance of a same-day visit, especially during peak summer.

How often should I get a tune-up?

At least once a year — ideally before summer. In Florida, where your AC runs most of the year, twice a year (spring and fall) keeps your system running its best.

Do you offer financing for installations?

Yes — 0% financing is available for qualified homeowners through trusted third-party lenders. Pre-qualifying is a soft credit pull and won't affect your score.
