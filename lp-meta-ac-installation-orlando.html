Is It Time?

Signs Your AC May Need Replacing

If your system is doing any of these, replacement is usually the smarter long-term move.

10+ Years Old

Most AC systems lose major efficiency after 10–15 years. Repairs only delay the inevitable.

Rising Energy Bills

An aging AC works harder to cool your home. Bills creep up month after month — even if usage doesn't.

Frequent Repairs

If you're calling a tech every season, replacement is usually cheaper than chasing the next breakdown.

Uses R-22 Refrigerant

R-22 (Freon) has been phased out. Recharging older systems is expensive — and often not worth it.

Uneven Cooling

Hot and cold spots in your home usually mean an undersized or failing system that can't keep up.

Loud or Noisy Operation

Banging, grinding, or rattling means major components are failing. Often a sign you're past the repair window.
