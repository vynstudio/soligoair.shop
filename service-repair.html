Common AC Problems We Fix

AC Not Cooling

Low refrigerant, dirty coils, faulty compressor — we diagnose and fix the root cause, not just the symptom.

Water Leaks

Clogged drain lines, frozen evaporator coils, or damaged pans. We stop the leak and prevent water damage.

Strange Noises

Grinding, squealing, or banging? These sounds signal worn parts that need immediate attention.

Short Cycling

AC turning on and off frequently wastes energy and wears out your system. We find and fix the cause.

Weak Airflow

Blocked ducts, failing blower motors, or dirty filters reducing comfort throughout your home.

High Electric Bills

A struggling AC drives up costs. Our tune-up and repair services restore peak efficiency.
