Common AC Problems

Symptoms We Fix Same-Day

If your AC is doing any of these, we can usually diagnose and repair it the same day you call.

AC Not Cooling

Refrigerant issues, compressor failure, or thermostat problems. Diagnosed today.

No Power / Won't Start

Tripped breaker, blown capacitor, or wiring issue. We trace and fix it fast.

Strange Noises

Grinding, squealing, banging — usually a failing motor or loose part. We find it.

Water Leaks

Clogged drain lines or frozen coils. We stop the leak and fix the root cause.

Weak Airflow

Dirty filter, blocked ducts, or failing blower motor. We restore full airflow.

High Electric Bills

A struggling AC spikes your bill. We find the inefficiency and fix it fast.
