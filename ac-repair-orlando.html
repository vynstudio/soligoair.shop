Common AC Problems We Fix

Most issues are diagnosed and repaired the same day you call.

AC Not Cooling

Thermostat issues, refrigerant leaks, compressor failure — we diagnose and fix it the same day.

Water Leaks

Clogged drain lines, frozen coils, or pan overflow. We stop the leak and fix the root cause.

Strange Noises

Grinding, squealing, or banging? Usually a failing motor, loose part, or worn belt. We'll find it fast.

Short Cycling

System turns on and off repeatedly? Could be a thermostat issue, refrigerant problem, or oversized unit.

Weak Airflow

Dirty filters, blocked ducts, or a failing blower motor. We restore full airflow quickly.

High Electric Bills

A struggling AC spikes your bill. We find the inefficiency and fix it so you stop overpaying.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you get here?

In most cases, we offer same-day service across Orlando and Central Florida. Call before noon for the best chance of a same-day visit.

How much does AC repair cost?

It depends on the issue. We charge a flat diagnostic fee, then provide an upfront quote before any work begins. No surprises.

Do you repair all AC brands?

Yes. Our technicians are trained on all major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, and Daikin.

Should I repair or replace my AC?

If your system is under 10 years old and the repair is less than 50% of a new system's cost, repair is usually the smart move. We'll give you an honest recommendation.
