What's Included in Our $89 Tune-Up

A full-system inspection and cleaning that keeps your AC running efficiently and prevents costly breakdowns.

Thermostat Calibration

We verify your thermostat reads correctly and cycles your system at the right intervals for consistent comfort.

Refrigerant Check

We measure refrigerant levels and check for leaks. Low refrigerant forces your system to work harder and costs more to run.

Coil Cleaning

Dirty coils reduce cooling capacity by up to 30%. We clean the evaporator and condenser coils to restore full efficiency.

Electrical Inspection

We tighten connections, measure voltage and amperage, and check capacitors — the most common cause of AC failure.

Drain Line Flush

Clogged drain lines cause water damage and mold. We flush the condensate drain to prevent backups.

Filter & Airflow Check

We inspect your filter, check return and supply airflow, and make sure your ductwork isn't restricting performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a tune-up take?

A typical tune-up takes 45–60 minutes. Our technician will walk you through everything they find before leaving.

How often should I get a tune-up?

At least once a year — ideally before summer. In Florida, where your AC runs most of the year, twice a year (spring and fall) is recommended.

Is the $89 price really all-inclusive?

Yes. The $89 covers the full inspection and cleaning. If we find an issue that needs a separate repair, we'll quote it upfront before doing anything extra.

What if you find a problem during the tune-up?
