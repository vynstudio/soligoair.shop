Why Replace Your AC Now

Waiting costs more than you think. An aging system wastes energy, breaks down more often, and costs more to repair.

10–15

Years Is the Limit

Most AC systems lose significant efficiency after 10–15 years. If yours is older, it's working harder and costing more.

50%

The Repair Rule

If a single repair costs more than 50% of a new system, replacement is the smarter investment every time.

R-22

Freon Phase-Out

R-22 refrigerant is no longer manufactured. If your system uses it, recharging is extremely expensive.

30%

Energy Savings

A new high-efficiency system can reduce your cooling costs by up to 30% compared to a 15-year-old unit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical AC replacement take?

Most residential replacements are completed in one day. Commercial projects typically take 1–3 days depending on the scope. We schedule most installations within the same week as your assessment.

What brands do you install?

We install all major brands: Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin, and more. We'll recommend the best option based on your needs, budget, and home or building layout.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes. We pull all required building permits and coordinate inspections with your local municipality. Your installation will be fully code-compliant.

How does financing work?

We offer 0% interest financing for qualifying customers. During your assessment, we'll walk you through available payment plans so you can choose what fits your budget.

Do you serve commercial properties?

Yes. We handle commercial HVAC installations for offices, retail, restaurants, warehouses, and multi-tenant buildings across Central Florida.
