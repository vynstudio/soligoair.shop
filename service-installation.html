The Right System, Installed Right

Whether you're building a new home, renovating an existing property, or installing central air conditioning for the first time, Soligo Air ensures you get the perfect system for your space. Our Installation Process

1Home Assessment & Load Calculation

We evaluate your home's square footage, insulation, windows, orientation, and occupancy to determine the exact cooling capacity you need using Manual J calculations.

2System Recommendation & Quote

Based on your assessment, we recommend the best system options for your budget and comfort goals — with clear, upfront pricing and no hidden fees.

3Professional Installation

Our licensed technicians install your system to manufacturer specifications with proper duct connections, refrigerant charging, and electrical work — all to Florida code.

4Testing, Training & Warranty

We test every component, walk you through thermostat operation and filter maintenance, and register your full manufacturer warranty for complete peace of mind.

Why Choose Soligo Air for Installation

Energy Efficient Systems

We install high-SEER systems that reduce energy consumption and lower your monthly utility bills — critical in Florida's year-round cooling climate.

Proper Duct Design

Correct ductwork sizing and layout ensures even airflow to every room — eliminating hot spots, humidity problems, and wasted energy.

Code Compliance & Permits

Every installation includes proper permitting and passes Florida building inspections. We handle all the paperwork so you don't have to.
